## Key Vocabulary & Professional Usage

- Cisnormativity (n.): The assumption that everyone's gender identity matches their sex assigned at birth.
- Legitimacy (n.): The quality of being accepted as valid, professional, or authoritative.
- Structural Literacy (n.): The ability to recognize and analyze the systemic patterns of power.

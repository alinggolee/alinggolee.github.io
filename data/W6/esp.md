# Week 6 ｜多樣性、性別與教養 (Diversity, Gender, and Parenting)

## ESP 學習目標 (ESP ILOs)

After this lesson, students can:

Use key words about gender（性別）, parenting（教養）, and social norms（社會常規）.

Use simple sentences to explain social expectations（社會期待） and power（權力）.

Write a short four-step reflection（四步驟反思）.

Practice inclusive language（包容性語言） in discussion.

## Reflection Questions

Have you ever noticed the look（眼光） strangers give
when a parent does not fit the traditional family image（傳統家庭形象）?

Where does this silent pressure（無聲壓力） come from?

If you were in their shoes,

would that pressure make you want to hide（隱藏自己）?

Or would it inspire you to redefine home（重新定義家庭）?

| Target Vocabulary        | Example                                                                                                             |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Gender Policing（性別監控）    | Society sometimes uses **gender policing（性別監控）** when people criticize parents who do not follow traditional roles. |
| Moral Panic（道德恐慌）        | Fear of diverse families can create **moral panic（道德恐慌）** in society.                                               |
| Protective Factors（保護因子） | Community support can be a **protective factor（保護因子）** for marginalized families.                                   |

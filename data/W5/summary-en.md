## Objective

By the end of this lesson, we will achieve the following:

- Distinguish multiple forms of attraction (sexual, romantic, aesthetic, sensual, emotional) using precise academic terminology.
- Analyze how sexual orientation is socially constructed, regulated, and frequently misinterpreted through heteronormative assumptions.
- Explain the structural relationship between gender norms and attraction without conflating gender identity, expression, and sexual orientation.

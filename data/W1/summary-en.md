## Objective

By the end of this lesson, we will achieve the following:
- **Understand** that gender is socially constructed rather than purely biological.
- **Identify** common gender stereotypes in daily life and media.
- **Use** basic English vocabulary to describe gender-related concepts and reflect on fairness.

## Course Content

In this lesson, we will explore how gender is socially constructed. We'll learn the four dimensions of the SOGIESC framework, how to identify hidden gender stereotypes in daily life and children's books, and reflect on the gender expectations that might be tied to our names.

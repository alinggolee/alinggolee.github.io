## Objective

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

1.	Identify and apply professional ethics in gender education facilitation.
2.	Analyze and interpret board game mechanics as specific representations of digital gender-based violence.
3.	Design and evaluate reflective questions and assessment tools for community-based teaching.



## Course Content

Advocacy & Educational Design: Facilitating "Ready for an Adventure!"

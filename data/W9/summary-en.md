## Objective

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

- Analyze how cultural gender norms operate within the framework of Gaya.
- Identify moments of cultural reinterpretation and individual resistance.
- Examine how legitimacy and authority shift across generations.
- Apply structural theory to analyze personal family and community norms.

## Course Content

Tradition, Change, and Gendered Identity (B1-B2 Level)

## ESP

The Language of Adaptation and Personal Agency

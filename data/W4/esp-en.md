## Key Vocabulary & Practical Application

- Media Amplification (n.): How media makes a specific idea or reaction much louder or bigger.
  - Ex: "The media amplification of this joke reinforced an old gender stereotype".
- Structural Bias (n.): Unfairness that is built into the way a system or media works.
  - Ex: "To fix this, we need to address the structural bias in the storytelling."
- Critical Framework (n.): A set of steps (like Describe and Analyze) used to think deeply.
  - Ex: "We applied the four-step critical framework to the viral video".
## TEEMI Speaking Prep (The Analysis Mission)

To improve your professional English for the TEEMI task, practice these B1-B2 level "Analysis Frames":

- Observation: "I described the clip, and I noticed a pattern of ridicule toward..."
- Structural Link: "This represents structural bias because the media is amplifying only one perspective."
- Vision: "My vision for change is to promote more inclusive representation through education."

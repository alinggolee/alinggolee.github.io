## Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

- Recognize how gender norms and power operate in viral events and media narratives.
- Apply a four-step critical thinking framework (Describe → Analyze → Build Vision → Develop Action).
- Move from individual reaction to structural understanding of gender regulation in society.
